About us

The College of Engineering Chengannur commonly known as CEC, is an engineering institute in the state of Kerala,India That was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) in 1993. The college is located in Chengannur, Alappuzha,The college is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and the courses are recognised by AICTE and accredited by NBA, the National Board of Accreditation, India The college was set under the auspices of the institute of human resourses and development
